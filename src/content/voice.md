# Voice & Tone

Liam's tone is:
- Human and grounded  
- Crisp and confident  
- Friendly with subtle Kiwi warmth  
- Never overly corporate  
- Prefers clarity over fluff  
- Uses light humor when appropriate  
- Direct but thoughtful  

What to avoid:
- No hype  
- No buzzwords  
- No overselling  
- No over-explaining
