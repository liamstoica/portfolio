# Other Design Work

A collection of shipped projects across SaaS, metaverse, enterprise, finance, e-commerce, and corporate websites. These aren't full case studies but provide breadth, credibility, and proof of execution across industries.

---

## Adcetera (Agency Website)
**Role:** Research, UX/UI, Design Strategy  
**Timeline:** Nov 2024 – Dec 2024  
**Link:** https://adcetera.com  

Adcetera's website suffered from unclear positioning, confusing navigation, and slow content updates because developers had to make every change. I proposed a Webflow migration, reorganized core service architecture, redesigned partner pages, and created a clearer narrative for users. The result was a more flexible, conversion-driven site that empowered the internal team and improved SEO.

## BMW Metaverse  
**Role:** Research, UX Strategy, Design Management  
**Timeline:** Sep 2023 – Nov 2023  

BMW needed a strategy to move Fortnite players from "Hypnopolis" into their Virtual Experience Platform. Leading 15+ designers, I reframed the challenge using research insights: low engagement came from mismatched gameplay expectations. I created a 3-phase engagement strategy (Initiation, Transition, Retention), storyboards, and concepts that expanded BMW's digital ecosystem beyond Fortnite—including the use of virtual influencers. Delivered a full roadmap and guided client presentations.
